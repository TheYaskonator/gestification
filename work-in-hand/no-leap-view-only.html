Jaanga / Gestification
~ apps for the Leap Motion device

These links will allow you to get to the Leap Motion apps even if you do not have the Leap Motion device. All the apps are to help programmers learn about th device.

Gestification ~ home page

You Gest ~ This app explores the Leap Motion API with the incoming numeric data represented as 3D objects.

Touchly ~ Point your finger at the cubes and watch them pop.

Show Of Hands ~ See your virtual hands - all seven of them.

Murgatroyd Heaven ~ The first app we made for LM.

Phalanges ~ The goal is to display two joints per finger.

Toolbar Test ~ Frequently used functions in a library file.

Touch Placards ~ Explores the use of two hands.

Hold 'Em ~ The beginnings of a 3D slider bar

Save JSON ~ Save your gestures to a file.
